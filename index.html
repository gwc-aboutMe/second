So, Who IS Elizabeth, Anyway?

Hello! My name is Elizabeth Bartusiak. My favorite colors are orange and yellow because they are so bright and happy! Something you would not guess by looking at me is that I love to play basketball. Consequently, I am also a big fan of the Chicago Bulls. I play the violin and love to practice often. Last year, I even played for a wedding! Aside from running on the basketball court and making music, I love math. To me, math is like solving complex problems using nothing but a little bit of hope and a lot of shear logic! In the future, you will find me working with computer science to protect other people's cyber-security. But I also have a creative side...
